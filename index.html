Flexibility is empowering

12 month contracts kill sponteneity. Why can't we jump on that one-way flight deal and work from Lisbon for a month? Why turn down a face-to-face chat with a client, when you can always come back to a home that's waiting for you?

CoHome's invested years in tailoring a structure that works for you, whether you're looking to stay for a couple of nights, a month, or as long as it takes to settle into your UK headquarters.

Next

But homeliness was not forgotten

With all the flexibility this nomadic lifestyle brings, we didn't want to lose that feeling of home. Of being with our people, for months or just a meal.

That's why we incentivise our flatmates to give back to the community, to take an active interest in growing each other - because together we can all succeed.

Next

Vision

Our vision is to create a sustainable coliving concept where everyone is invested in personal growth and in helping others grow themselves; whether that be on the social, financial, professional, experiential, spiritual, or any other axis.

This is a community of people who love hearing about each other's lives and openly offer up their own experience to help other people grow. It's a community of change.

We've created a place where light-footed househoppers (and globetrotters!) can take their passions home with them when they want to. Because sharing your passion with the browser or a blank-faced flatmate can suck.

If you define a great company as one that prioritises developing you as a person, why not choose a home that grows you too?
